DC Crime Map

Access this page to see where crime is concentrated around the city.




Violent crime in DC has gone down 53.3% since 1995, according to the "Uniform Crime Report" conducted by the FBI. However, violent crime is stll three times the national average. While violent crime still plagues the city, administrators are working to mitigate the violent crime rate.




DC Crime Occurrences Over the Year

Access this page to see which months crime occurs more frequently in each ward.




In Washington DC, all crime and violent crime happens across all wards, or sections, of the city. The highest concentration of ALL crime is in Ward 2, however, when you observe only violent crime, Ward 7 and Ward 8 have the most occurrences of crime. Ward 3 has the least concentration of violent crime. When observing the time of year, October is the month with the most crime, while February has the least.




DC Crime Threat Analysis

Access this page to see what times of day have the highest concentration of crime.




A time wheel is a visualization used readily in the military to assess threats. Our time wheel analyzes the time of day that has the most crime. Our analysis found some unexpected trends.




DC Crime Machine Learning

Access this page to learn about the machine learning process we used to predict crime in DC.




The goal of the machine learning portion of our project is to examine possible relationships and correlations between violent crime and socioeconomic conditions in Washington, D.C. While much of our overall application is devoted to dynamic visualizations of crime data across the city, we aim to apply machine learning in a way that attempts to explain what might drive the patterns of violent crime observed in the visualizations.
